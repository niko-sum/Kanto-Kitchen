Plant-based Filipinx cafe and restaurant
in the heart of historic East Valley

Okay, so who are we?

Just a bunch of East Valley kids who love home a bit too much and ended up with an award-winning restaurant. What can we say? We wanted to create a third space for our community and travelers alike. Think "your tita's living room" but with even bomber food (yes we said it).

Filipinx-inspired drinks

Our signature Ube Latte will blow your socks off. Also try our Carabao Mango Milk Tea,
Pandan Coconut Frappucino, and more.

Fresh and local ingredients

We strive to use sustainable local ingredients to whip you up the freshest dishes.
Dip our our award-winning Crispy Chik'n Lumpia in our house spiced vinegar. Did someone say extra rice?

Karaoke night at Kanto

Vocal chops not required!

If you're in the mood to tap your singing groove then make your way to Kanto and join on the fun. Saturdays starting at 6pm. Don't let the "All Filipino's can sing" stereotype scare you away - you won't see it 10% of the time.
